Danish Pastries

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 packages active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cold butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Fruit or cheese filling

Instructions

  1. Make laminated dough
  2. Shape into desired forms
  3. Add filling
  4. Let rise
  5. Brush with egg wash
  6. Bake at 400°F for 15-20 minutes

Classic French Croissants

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups cold unsalted butter for laminating

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add warm milk and melted butter, knead until smooth
  3. Rest dough for 1 hour
  4. Laminate with cold butter in three turns
  5. Shape and proof croissants
  6. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes

Cake Donuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Whisk wet ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed
  4. Roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness
  5. Cut into donut shapes
  6. Fry at 375°F until golden brown

Buttermilk Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons cold butter
  • 3/4 cup cold buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Cut in cold butter until crumbly
  3. Stir in buttermilk until just combined
  4. Pat and fold dough 5 times
  5. Cut into rounds
  6. Bake at 425°F for 12-15 minutes

Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients until just combined
  4. Fold in blueberries
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full
  6. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Make dough and let rise
  2. Roll out dough into rectangle
  3. Spread with butter, cinnamon, and sugar
  4. Roll up and slice
  5. Let rise again
  6. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes

Pecan Spiral Nut Rolls

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chopped pecans
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Prepare sweet dough
  2. Roll out and spread with filling
  3. Sprinkle with pecans
  4. Roll up and slice
  5. Let rise
  6. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes

Egg Sheet

Ingredients

  • 18 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional mix-ins (vegetables, herbs)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs and cream
  2. Add seasonings and mix-ins
  3. Pour into greased sheet pan
  4. Top with cheese
  5. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes
  6. Cut into squares to serve
